Our Story

Founded in 2010, Family Pets began as a small neighborhood pet store with a big heart. What started as a passion project for animal lovers has grown into the premier pet care destination in the region.

Our mission is simple: to provide exceptional care, quality products, and expert advice to help pet owners give their companions the happy, healthy lives they deserve.

Every member of our team shares a deep love for animals and undergoes rigorous training to ensure we meet the highest standards of pet care.
